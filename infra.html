Infrastructure of School

School Infra: The main structure where the school operates, including administrative offices, classrooms, and common areas.

Classrooms: Individual spaces for teaching and learning, equipped with desks, chairs, chalkboards or whiteboards, and audio-visual equipment.

Library: A dedicated space for students and teachers to access a wide range of books, reference materials, and digital resources.

Computer Labs and Science Labs: Specialized rooms equipped with computers for computer-based learning and laboratories for practical science lessons.

Playgrounds: Outdoor spaces for recreation, including playground equipment.
